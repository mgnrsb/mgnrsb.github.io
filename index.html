About Me

Hey, I'm Megan. I'm a front end developer and user researcher interested in creating a joyful user experience for a startup with a positive social impact.

My background is in psychology and education. I'm a recent graduate of the Product Design track of the Startup Institute in Chicago, an 8-week accelerated program to give us the skills and mindset for working in startups. Currently, I'm a UX Developer with an early-stage startup based out of Lightbank.

I love anything new, from people to food to travel to whatever you can put in a book. I'm passionate about giving back to the local community and diversity in technology.
